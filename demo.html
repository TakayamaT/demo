Diego Velazquez

About Velazquez

Diego Rodríguez de Silva y Velázquez who was a court painter of King Philip IV and Queen Mariana of Austria is one of the most famous painters in Spain. he was born in 1599 in Seville, Spain. When he was eleven years old, he was apprenciced to Francisco Pacheco, a Seville's artist. He was a painter of the Spanish royal court for over 40 years. He died in 1660 in Madrid, Spain. His most famous painting is Las Meninas (Prado Museum) that is painted royal families. The center of it is drawing Princess Margaret Theresa.

My favorite paintings are Las Meninas and The Rokeby Venus.
